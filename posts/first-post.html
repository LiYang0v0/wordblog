第一篇博客 First Post

这里是第一篇博客的正文。它不用很完整，也不用一开始就像正式文章。个人博客最好的地方，是可以先作为一个安静的地方存在。

之后你可以把读书笔记、研究之外的碎念、旅行、电影、播客、生活观察，都慢慢放进来。

This is the body of the first post. It does not need to be complete, and it does not need to sound like a finished essay from the beginning. The best thing about a personal blog is that it can first exist as a quiet place.

Later, reading notes, stray thoughts outside research, travel, films, podcasts, and ordinary observations can slowly find their way here.
